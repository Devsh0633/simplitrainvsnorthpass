Frequently Asked Questions

Questions training teams ask when comparing SimpliTrain and Northpass side by side.

What is the main difference between SimpliTrain and Northpass?
The difference is fundamental. Northpass (now Gainsight Customer Education) is a customer education platform built for SaaS companies who want to teach their own customers how to use a software product. It delivers self-paced digital content through a branded academy and has no concept of session scheduling, instructor management, or live training delivery. SimpliTrain is a Training Management System that handles the full operational lifecycle of instructor-led training, including ILT scheduling, vILT delivery, trainer management, cohort management, compliance reporting, and learning delivery for both self-paced and live programs. The two platforms were built for different jobs. If you run training programs with instructors, sessions, and live delivery, SimpliTrain is the tool built for that work.
Does Northpass support instructor-led training (ILT) or virtual instructor-led training (vILT)?
No. Northpass does not support ILT or vILT in any form. There is no session scheduling, no trainer management, no room allocation, no training calendar, and no live virtual classroom. The platform is built entirely around self-paced digital content delivered through a branded academy. If your training operation includes live classroom sessions or virtual instructor-led sessions, Northpass cannot manage them and has no integration path to bring that data back into the same system. SimpliTrain was built with ILT and vILT at its core, handling scheduling, delivery, and learner records in one platform.
How does Northpass pricing compare to SimpliTrain?
Northpass does not publish pricing. Based on independent review platforms and third-party reports, custom quotes typically land between $20,000 and $80,000 per year for standalone use. When bundled with the broader Gainsight Customer Success platform, total costs can exceed $100,000 per year. Opaque pricing is consistently flagged as a complaint in user reviews. SimpliTrain publishes its pricing openly on the website, charges a flat rate per admin user, and includes unlimited learners with no variable fees. You can evaluate the cost before speaking to anyone in sales.
Is Northpass still a standalone product or has it been merged into Gainsight?
Northpass was acquired by Gainsight in July 2023 and rebranded as Gainsight Customer Education. In April 2025, Gainsight acquired Skilljar, another external training LMS, and the two products are now being consolidated under the Gainsight education umbrella. The northpass.com domain redirects to Gainsight. For teams evaluating Northpass today, the product is in an active transition period, with feature consolidation and roadmap direction still being determined across the merged platforms. SimpliTrain is an independent platform with a stable, focused roadmap for instructor-led and virtual training teams.
Can SimpliTrain replace both a Training Management System and an LMS in one platform?
Yes. SimpliTrain combines TMS and LMS functionality in a single platform. On the TMS side: session scheduling, instructor management, room and resource allocation, cohort management, multi-location operations, and compliance reporting. On the LMS side: self-paced eLearning delivery, SCORM and xAPI support, learning paths, course authoring, assessments, and certification management. Both sides share the same learner records and reporting, so there is no integration to maintain and no data reconciliation needed between scheduling and delivery. For most training teams, SimpliTrain replaces both a TMS and a separate LMS in a single migration.
Can I use SimpliTrain to sell online courses and training programs commercially?
Yes. SimpliTrain includes a public course catalog, online registration, payment processing, B2B invoicing, subscription billing, and multi-tenant client portals. Commercial training providers can manage fee-based external programs and internal L&D from the same platform. Northpass is oriented toward a single-product customer academy model and is not designed for open enrollment, commercial course sales, or multi-client billing. If you want to sell online courses to external learners alongside your instructor-led programs, SimpliTrain covers both workflows natively.
Does SimpliTrain have a mobile app?
Yes. SimpliTrain provides a native mobile app for both learners and training coordinators. Learners can access course content, join virtual sessions, and view certificates from any device. Training coordinators can manage session logistics, check instructor schedules, and mark attendance from on-site without needing a desktop. Northpass does not have a dedicated mobile app and relies on mobile browser access only, which is a frequently mentioned limitation in user reviews for teams whose coordinators manage training away from a desk.
